Orbital speed

$$ \begin{aligned} \text{average orbital speed} & = \frac{\text{orbit distance}}{\text{time taken for one orbit}}\\ \implies \text{average orbital speed} & = \frac{2 \pi r}{t} \\ \end{aligned}$$

* The lengths of the hour and minute hands on a clock are $6cm$ and $8cm$. Calculate the average orbital speed of each of the hands.
* Calculate the average orbital speed of the Moon, given that the average distance of the Moon from the Earth to be $380 000 \\;km$ and the orbital period of the Moon to be 27 days.

### 9.12: Power in our country One of the problems in distribution of electricity is the loss of power due to the resistance of the wire. Possible solutions: * Use very thick cables so that the resistance is low. * Reduce the magnitude of the current flowing in the cables. * This can be done with a step-up transformer.
